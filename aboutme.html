About Me



My four favorite movies are:


  1. The Big Lebowski
  2. Major League
  3. Bull Durham
  4. Ace Ventura: Pet Detective

My name is Shaun. I was born in 1988 in a small Missouri town called Warrensburg.

I moved away in 2009 when I enlisted in the U.S. Air Force. I was assigned to Lackland AFB in TX, Keesler AFB in MS, Altus AFB in OK, and Andersen AFB on the remote island of Guam.

In all, I spent more than seven years in the service as an Air Traffic Controller. I spent another year-plus in the same profession as a civilian.

I am 30 years old now and working on my third career. Armed with very little (basically zero) experience with the field, I chose to pursue a career in Computer Programming.
